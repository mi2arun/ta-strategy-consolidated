01 · Our Vision

Moving from reactive hiring to a proactive system

Currently we hire only after a role opens. We will shift to planning hiring ahead of demand — based on the store-expansion roadmap — and keep pre-screened candidates ready. The focus moves to hiring quality talent that stays, not simply filling roles quickly.

🎯 Our four focus areas

  • Speed — talent pipelines + AI + clear timelines. Goal: hire within 30 days.
  • Quality — structured interviews + an honest role preview. Goal: new hires staying past 90 days.
  • Cost-efficiency — more referrals, fewer agencies, AI support. Goal: cost per hire ₹4,500.
  • Employer brand — a consistent social presence + the Apple brand advantage. Goal: higher offer acceptance.
02 · First 90 Days

The first 90 days — a phased plan

Sequenced deliberately — a broken process cannot be scaled. First fix the basics, then build the system, then demonstrate results.

0–30Assess & Stabilise
  • Review open roles, time taken at each stage, sources of hire & attrition by store.
  • Move all hiring onto a single applicant tracking system (ATS).
  • Dedicated team for the 100 new-store roles.
  • Relaunch the employee referral programme with prompt payouts.
31–60Build the System
  • Standardised interview kits + culture-fit scorecards.
  • Maintain pre-screened talent pipelines for high-volume roles.
  • Redesign onboarding: ready from Day 1, with 30/60/90-day check-ins.
  • Introduce AI screening + chatbot; reduce agency dependence.
61–90Demonstrate Results
  • Early wins: time-to-hire ~40 days; fewer offer declines.
  • Launch employer-branding content.
  • Go live with a real-time hiring dashboard for leadership.
  • Present the full 12-month roadmap.

🎯 Our three-part approach

  • A · Hire right — assess culture fit, give an honest role preview, and raise referrals to 40%+ (they stay longer).
  • B · Onboard well — ready Day 1, assign a buddy, review at 30/60/90 days. One owner for first-90-day retention.

07 · Technology & Cost

AI drives speed — and reduces cost by half

AI handles the repetitive work — sourcing, screening, scheduling and candidate queries. Cost falls mainly through more referrals, fewer agencies, and fewer re-hires.

🤖 Technology stack — phased

  • Phase 1 — full ATS + live dashboards (the data foundation).
  • Phase 2 — AI screening + a WhatsApp/chat assistant for high-volume roles.
  • Phase 3 — AI sourcing + early attrition warning signals.

💰 Cost: ₹9,000 → ₹4,500

  • Referrals 20% → 40%+ — the lowest-cost channel, with better retention.
  • Agencies 9–10% → under 4% — bring sourcing & leadership hiring in-house.
  • AI automation — lower cost per candidate at high volume.
  • Lower attrition — each avoided replacement saves a full hiring cost + training time.
🔁 A positive cycle: referrals stay longer → attrition falls → fewer re-hires → recruiters focus on growth rather than replacement → agency budget shifts to lower-cost, higher-quality channels.
