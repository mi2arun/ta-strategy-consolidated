01 · Our Vision

From a slow hiring desk to a smart hiring system

Today we hire only after a job opens. We will change this — plan hiring early from new-store plans, and keep candidates ready in advance. We will hire good people who stay longer, not just fill seats fast.

🎯 Our four focus areas

  • Speed — ready candidate lists + AI + clear timelines. Goal: hire in 30 days.
  • Quality — proper interview scorecards + honest job preview. Goal: joinees stay past 90 days.
  • Low cost — more referrals, fewer agencies, AI support. Goal: cost per hire ₹4,500.
  • Brand — regular social posts + Apple brand value. Goal: more offers accepted.
02 · First 90 Days

First 90 days — a step-by-step plan

Done in order — we cannot speed up a broken process. First fix the basics, then build the system, then show results.

0–30Understand & Fix Basics
  • Check open jobs, time at each step, source of hires & who is leaving (store-wise).
  • Make everyone use one hiring software (ATS).
  • Daily focus team for the 100 new-store jobs.
  • Restart referral scheme with instant reward.
31–60Build the System
  • Standard interview questions + culture-fit scorecards.
  • Keep ready, pre-checked candidate lists.
  • New-joinee plan: ready Day 1 + check-ins at 30/60/90 days.
  • Start AI screening + chatbot; reduce agency contracts.
61–90Show Results
  • Early wins: hiring time ~40 days; fewer offer drop-outs.
  • Start employer-branding posts.
  • Launch a live hiring dashboard for leadership.
  • Present the full 12-month plan.
03 · 12-Month Plan

The 12-month plan in four quarters

Each quarter builds on the last. We finish one quarter's base work (the gate) before starting the next.

Q1Foundation
  • Hiring software fully used · standard interviews · referral scheme · new-joinee plan. Gate: ATS used by >90%.
Q2Speed
  • AI screening at scale · ready lists for top 5 jobs · time limits per step. Gate: hiring time ≤35 days.
Q3Quality & Brand
  • Employer-branding push · leadership hiring guide · career paths live. Gate: people leaving <35%.
Q4Optimise
  • Reduce cost & agencies · plan hiring in advance. Gate: 30 days · ₹4,500 · agency <4%.

🎯 How we make hiring faster

  • Keep ready candidate lists for high-volume jobs → fill in days, not weeks.
  • Run steps together — screening, interview & booking at once; AI screens 24×7.
  • Fixed time limits — manager feedback in 2 days, offer within 5 days.

🎯 Our three-step plan

07 · Technology & Cost

AI gives speed — and cuts the cost in half

AI handles the repeated work — searching, screening, scheduling and answering candidate questions. Cost mainly drops from more referrals, fewer agencies, and fewer re-hires.

🤖 Hiring tools — step by step

  • Step 1 — full hiring software (ATS) + live dashboards.
  • Step 2 — AI screening + a WhatsApp/chat assistant for high-volume jobs.
  • Step 3 — AI candidate search + early alerts on who may leave.

💰 Cost: ₹9,000 → ₹4,500

  • Referrals 20% → 40%+ — cheapest channel, and people stay longer.
  • Agencies 9–10% → under 4% — do sourcing & leadership hiring in-house.
  • AI automation — lower cost per candidate at high volume.
  • Less leaving — each replacement avoided saves a full hiring cost + training time.
🔁 The good cycle: referrals stay longer → fewer people leave → fewer re-hires → recruiters focus on growth, not replacement → agency money moves to cheaper, better channels.

⚠ Risk → What we do

  • Competitors hiring fast → strong brand + quicker offers + referrals.
  • Speed vs quality → ready candidate lists + proper assessment.
  • Attrition not falling → focus first on joining & the first 90 days.
  • Slow software adoption → make ATS compulsory; link to manager reviews.
  • Sudden demand → 100-hire buffer + ready candidate lists.
