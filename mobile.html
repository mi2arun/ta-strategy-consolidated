🪣 The leaky bucket LEAK

45% attrition · 55%+ frontline · 11-mo tenure. ~55% of hiring just replaces churn instead of fuelling growth.

🐢 Reactive & slow SLOW

50+ day TAT, weak forecasting, reactive pipelines. Stores open understaffed; panic hiring lowers quality.

💸 Expensive & manual COST

₹9,000/hire · 9–10% agency spend · partial ATS · no AI/analytics · only 20% referral hiring.

The engine was built for steady-state replacement at ₹3,000 Cr. The three problems feed a vicious cycle: high attrition forces volume → volume overwhelms a slow process → slowness forces premium agency hiring → rushed, mismatched hires churn again. Breaking the attrition link collapses the whole cycle.

🎯 How we solve it

  • Attack attrition first — every retained employee is one fewer replacement hire, shrinking both the ~1,000 number and its cost.
  • Convert ~820 frontline roles to always-on talent pools so demand spikes are absorbed from a warm bench, not panic-hired.
  • Instrument the funnel end-to-end (ATS + analytics) — manage by data, not gut feel.
  • Re-channel agency spend into referrals & in-house sourcing — cut cost and raise quality at once.

03 · Vision

From reactive hiring desk to talent engine

"The right people, faster, cheaper, and with staying power — a proactive, data-driven, brand-led talent engine."

The shift is from a function that fills requisitions after they open to one that forecasts demand from the store-expansion plan and builds talent ahead of need — a workforce-planning partner to Retail Ops, measured on quality and tenure, not just speed.

🎯 What each pillar means

  • Speed → pipelines + AI + SLAs (TA Ops). Metric: predictable ≤30-day TAT.
  • Quality & Stickiness → culture-fit scorecards + realistic job previews. Metric: 90-day stick rate.
  • Efficiency → 40%+ referrals, <4% agency, AI automation. Metric: cost-per-hire ₹4,500.
  • Employer Brand → always-on content + Apple-ecosystem prestige. Metric: inbound applications & offer-accept rate.

07 · Faster Hiring

Cutting TAT from 50+ to ≤30 days

First diagnose where the 50 days goes: the funnel stalls when sourcing starts from zero on every role and while waiting on hiring-manager feedback. The fix is structural — pre-build candidates and run the funnel in parallel under hard SLAs.

🎯 The concrete TAT plan

  • Pipeline, not posting: pre-screened pools for ~820 volume roles → days, not weeks.
  • Parallel funnel: screen, assess & schedule concurrently; AI screens 24/7.
  • SLA-locked stages: HM feedback in 48h · interview-to-offer in 5 days, with escalation.
  • Instant scheduling via chatbot for frontline; standing exec-search bench for leadership.
  • Cut offer-drop 28%→<12% with faster cycles, transparent comp & warm engagement to Day 1.

🎯 The three-stage framework

  • A · Hire right: culture-fit & realistic-job-preview screening + 40%+ referrals → fewer mismatched early-quitters.
  • B · Onboard right: Day-1 readiness, buddy system, 30/60/90 check-ins, a named owner for the 90-day stick rate.

09 · Technology, AI & Cost

AI drives the speed — and halves the cost

AI absorbs the repetitive, high-volume work (sourcing, screening, scheduling, FAQs) that today eats recruiter time and agency fees, so the same team handles more hires at lower cost. Cost falls mainly as a by-product of more referrals, fewer agencies, and less rework from lower attrition.

🤖 Phased tech rollout

  • Phase 1: full ATS + live dashboards (data spine).
  • Phase 2: AI screening + conversational chatbot on volume funnels.
  • Phase 3: AI sourcing + predictive attrition analytics.

💰 Cost levers: ₹9,000 → ₹4,500

  • Referrals 20%→40%+ via instant-payout scheme — cheapest, stickiest channel.
  • Agency 9–10%→<4% by in-sourcing sourcing & leadership search.
  • Lower attrition — the largest hidden saving: every avoided replacement saves a full hire + ramp-up.
🔁 The flywheel: referrals stay longer → attrition falls → fewer re-hires → recruiters focus on growth, not churn → agency budget reallocated to cheaper, stickier channels.
