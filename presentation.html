1 The leaky bucket LEAK

45% attrition, 55%+ frontline, 11-month tenure. ~55% of hiring just replaces churn instead of fuelling growth.

2 Reactive & slow SLOW

50+ day TAT, weak forecasting, reactive pipelines. Stores open understaffed; panic hiring lowers quality.

3 Expensive & manual COST

₹9,000/hire, 9–10% agency spend, partial ATS, no AI/analytics, only 20% referral hiring.

💡 Core insight: the three problems feed a vicious cycle — high attrition forces volume → volume overwhelms a slow process → slowness forces premium agency hiring → mismatched hires churn again. Breaking the attrition link collapses the whole cycle.

Execution · First 90 Days
04 / 12

A sequenced 30 · 60 · 90 day plan

Deliberately ordered — you cannot scale a broken process. Stabilise and instrument first, build the engine second, prove results third. Early visible wins buy credibility for the deeper fixes.

🟦 Days 0–30 · Diagnose & Stabilize

  • Audit open reqs, TAT by stage, source-of-hire & attrition by store/tenure.
  • Mandate full ATS adoption — one source of truth.
  • War-room for the 100 new-store buffer roles.
  • Relaunch referrals with instant payouts.

🟩 Days 31–60 · Build the Engine

  • Structured interview kits + culture-fit scorecards.
  • Always-on talent pools for high-volume roles.
  • Onboarding redesign: Day-1 readiness + 30/60/90 check-ins.
  • Deploy AI screening + chatbot; cut consultant contracts.

🟨 Days 61–90 · Scale & Prove

  • Early wins: TAT ~40 days on volume roles; offer-drop <20%.
  • Launch employer-brand content.
  • Go live with the real-time KPI dashboard.
  • Present the committed 12-month roadmap to leadership.

🎯 The concrete TAT plan

  • Pipeline, not posting — pre-screened pools for ~820 volume roles → days, not weeks.
  • Parallel funnel — screen, assess & schedule concurrently; AI screens 24/7.

🎯 The three-stage framework

  • A · Hire right — culture-fit & realistic-job-preview screening + 40%+ referrals (referred hires stay longer).
  • B · Onboard right — Day-1 readiness, buddy system, 30/60/90 check-ins, a named owner for the 90-day stick rate.

  • Sequence — target the 55%+ frontline and 11-month tenure cliff first for fastest impact.
Technology, AI & Cost
09 / 12

AI drives the speed — and halves the cost

AI absorbs the repetitive, high-volume work (sourcing, screening, scheduling, FAQs) that eats recruiter time and agency fees. Cost falls mainly as a by-product of more referrals, fewer agencies, and less rework from lower attrition.

🤖 Recruitment tech stack — phased

  • Phase 1 — full ATS + live dashboards (the data spine).
  • Phase 2 — AI screening + conversational chatbot on volume funnels.
  • Phase 3 — AI sourcing + predictive attrition analytics.
  • Plus employer-brand programmatic ads & social distribution.

💰 Cost: ₹9,000 → ₹4,500

  • Referrals 20% → 40%+ — cheapest channel & highest retention.
  • Agency 9–10% → <4% — in-source sourcing & leadership search.
  • AI automation — lower cost-per-touch at volume.
  • Lower attrition — every avoided replacement saves a full hire + ramp-up.
🔁 The flywheel: referrals stay longer → attrition falls → fewer re-hires → recruiters focus on growth, not churn → agency budget reallocated to cheaper, stickier channels.
