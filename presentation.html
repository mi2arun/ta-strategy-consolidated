09 · Cost Optimization · Q4

Halving cost per hire: ₹9,000 → ₹4,500

💰 Where the savings come from

  • Referrals 20% → 40%+ — cheapest channel & highest retention.
  • Consultant 9–10% → <4% — in-house sourcing & leadership hiring.
  • AI automation — lower cost-per-touch on ~820 volume roles.
  • Lower attrition — every avoided replacement saves a full cost-of-hire plus ramp-up.

🔁 The flywheel effect

  • Referrals stay longer → attrition falls → fewer re-hires.
  • Fewer re-hires → recruiters focus on growth, not churn.
  • Reallocate agency budget into cheaper, stickier channels.
  • Brand + speed → lower offer-drop → less wasted spend.

11 · Risks & Mitigation

What could go wrong — and the answer

Key risks

  • Competitors hiring aggressively.
  • Scaling volume while raising the quality bar.
  • Attrition not dropping fast enough.
  • Slow technology adoption.
  • New-store demand spikes.

Mitigation

  • Employer brand + faster offers + referral pull.
  • Pipelines + structured assessment so speed ≠ lower quality.
  • Front-load onboarding & first-90-day fixes (fastest lever).
  • Mandate ATS use; tie to manager scorecards.
  • 100-hire buffer + always-on pools absorb surges.
