First 90 Days
02 / 08

First 90 days — a step-by-step plan

Done in order — we cannot speed up a broken process. First understand and fix the basics, then build the system, then show results. Quick early wins build trust for the bigger changes.

🟦 Days 0–30 · Understand & Fix Basics

  • Check all open jobs, time taken at each step, where hires come from, and who is leaving (store-wise).
  • Make everyone use one hiring software (ATS).
  • Daily focus team for the 100 new-store jobs.
  • Restart the referral scheme with instant reward payout.

🟩 Days 31–60 · Build the System

  • Standard interview questions + culture-fit scorecards.
  • Keep ready, pre-checked candidate lists for high-volume jobs.
  • New-joinee plan: ready from Day 1 + check-ins at 30, 60, 90 days.
  • Start AI screening + chatbot; reduce agency contracts.

🟨 Days 61–90 · Show Results

  • Early wins: hiring time ~40 days; fewer candidates dropping offers.
  • Start employer-branding posts.
  • Launch a live hiring dashboard for leadership.
  • Present the full 12-month plan to leadership.

Faster Hiring
05 / 08

Reduce hiring time from 50+ to 30 days or less

Hiring is slow because we start from zero for every job, and we wait too long for manager feedback. The fix is to keep candidates ready and run the steps together with clear time limits.

⭐ Example: Chipotle hires in 4 days while hiring 10,000/year — so our 30-day goal is easy to reach.

🎯 How we make hiring faster

  • Keep ready candidate lists for high-volume jobs (not fresh postings) → fill in days, not weeks.
  • Run steps together — screening, interview & booking at the same time; AI screens 24×7.
  • Fixed time limits — manager feedback in 2 days, offer within 5 days.
  • Instant interview booking by chatbot for store jobs; a ready panel for leadership roles.
  • Reduce offer drop-outs 28% → under 12% with a faster process and clear salary upfront.

🎯 Our three-step plan

  • A · Hire right — check culture fit, show an honest job preview, and use 40%+ referrals (referred people stay longer).
  • B · Settle them well — ready from Day 1, give a buddy, and check in at 30/60/90 days. One owner for first-90-day retention.
  • C · Keep them — a clear growth path (Advisor → Senior → Team Lead → Store Manager) + early alerts when someone may leave.
  • Start with store staff (55%+) and the 11-month problem first.
Technology & Cost
07 / 08

AI gives speed — and cuts the cost in half

AI handles the repeated work — searching, screening, scheduling and answering candidate questions — which today takes recruiter time and agency money. Cost mainly drops because of more referrals, fewer agencies, and fewer re-hires.

🤖 Hiring tools — step by step

  • Step 1 — full hiring software (ATS) + live dashboards (the data base).
  • Step 2 — AI screening + a WhatsApp/chat assistant for high-volume jobs.
  • Step 3 — AI candidate search + early alerts on who may leave.
  • Plus job ads on social media.

💰 Cost: ₹9,000 → ₹4,500

  • Referrals 20% → 40%+ — cheapest channel, and people stay longer.
  • Agencies 9–10% → under 4% — do sourcing & leadership hiring in-house.
  • AI automation — lower cost per candidate at high volume.
  • Less leaving — every replacement avoided saves a full hiring cost + training time.
🔁 The good cycle: referrals stay longer → fewer people leave → fewer re-hires → recruiters focus on growth, not replacement → agency money moves to cheaper, better channels.
